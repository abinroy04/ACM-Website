Rules and Regulations

24-Hour Website Creation Competition

Event Title:Web Canvas: Website Creation Hackathon
Duration: 24 Hours
Team Size: 2 - 4 Participants per Team

1. General Rules

  1. The competition is open to all eligible participants who have registered before the deadline.
  2. Each team must consist of 2 - 4 members. No individual participation is allowed.
  3. The competition will run for 24 hours continuously, and all submissions must be made before the deadline.
  4. The problem statement will be released at the beginning of the competition, and teams must adhere to the given requirements.
  5. All work must be original. Any form of plagiarism or copying existing websites will result in disqualification.
  6. External templates, themes, or pre-built website builders (such as WordPress, Wix, or Squarespace) are not allowed. However, teams may use frontend frameworks like React, Vue.js, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, or backend frameworks like Flask, Django, or Express.js.

2. Technical Rules

  1. Teams are free to use any programming languages and frameworks for frontend and backend development.
  2. The website must be responsive and functional across multiple devices.
  3. All assets must be royalty-free or self-created.

3. Judging Criteria

Criteria Weightage
Design & Aesthetics 20%
Functionality 20%
User Experience (UX) 20%
Technical Implementation 20%
Completion & Innovation 20%

4. Prizes & Recognition

  • First Place: Certificate of Achievement + INR 5000
  • Second Place: Certificate of Excellence + INR 2500
  • Third Place: Certificate of Merit
  • All Participants: Digital Certificate of Participation
